Privacy Policy – NFC Friend

Last updated: September 2025

NFC Friend does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app uses the NFC functionality of your device to read and write NFC tags locally. No tag data is uploaded to external servers.

The app displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy to provide personalized ads.

By using NFC Friend, you agree that the app only interacts with your device and does not process or store personal data beyond what is necessary for its functionality and advertising.

If you have any questions, contact us at: support@jessilab.com
