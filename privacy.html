Privacy Policy – Jessi Lab Applications

Last updated: September 2025

All Jessi Lab apps (including NFC Friend, Jessi Lab Kits, and others) do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Some apps use device features such as NFC, Bluetooth, or sensors. All data stays on the user’s device and is not uploaded to external servers.

Some apps display ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy to provide personalized ads.

By using Jessi Lab applications, you agree that they only interact with your device and do not process or store personal data beyond what is necessary for functionality and advertising.
